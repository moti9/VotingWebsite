Voting App is a web-based application that allows registered voters to cast their votes online. It provides a convenient and secure way for voters to participate in elections without having to physically go to a polling station.

Voting App uses advanced encryption and authentication techniques to ensure the security and integrity of the voting process. It also provides real-time monitoring and auditing of the voting results to prevent fraud and manipulation.
